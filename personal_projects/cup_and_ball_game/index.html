Cup And Ball Game (C++)

Dec 2023

Play a WASM Build!

How to Play

The first round is only the cup and ball game at the top. After the shuffling ends, use the arrow keys or AD to select the correct cup with the ball, and press SPACE to submit a guess

Subsequent rounds have both a minigame and the cup and ball game going at the same time. Use the arrow keys or WASD to complete the minigame. Once the minigame ends, the cups will stop shuffling and you are able to select the correct cup. Every time the wrong cup is guessed you lose a life. The goal of the game is to beat as many rounds as possible. Later rounds increase in difficulty by running faster and adding more cups.

About

This is a game jam project written for the theme "lost and found". The jam was 72 hours long, but two of the days were work days and I did not take any PTO, so I was pretty limited on time.

This program was my first time experimenting with compiling C++ to web assembly.
