About

Github project link

This game was made as an inside joke for a friend of mine who at the time really really liked Arizona iced tea. The game ended up being a fair bit larger than I originally planned, as I was having a lot of fun making it, and progress was a lot faster than I intially anticipated. Im pretty proud of how the boss fights turned out in particular.

With the exception of a few sprites made by a friend, all the art assets are photoshops of images I found on google images.
