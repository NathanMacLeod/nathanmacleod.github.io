Royal Game of Ur (C++)

Jul 2021

The royal game of ur is board game dating back to the early third millenium BC. If you are curious, I think the below video is an excellent and entertaining overview of the game.

Play a WASM Build!

[click here to toggle visbility]

Controls

On your turn, click anywhere to roll the dice. Then click the piece you would like the move, and then click where you would like to move it.

Rules

The rules follow those described as the "Finkel ruleset" here, the same as are seen in the Tom Scott video.

About

Github project link

I came across the video featuring Tom Scott and Irving Finkel, and thought it would be fun to try to write a bot that would play the game. And so I did! I think it plays not quite as good as a human, but it puts up a pretty game.
